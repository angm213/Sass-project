Workflows to Accelerate Your Success

Multiply Referrals Using Gamification

Now you can increase your volume of qualified referrals, and the new sales that they bring, using proven gamification workflows. Encourage referrals for specific products, whole categories, limited-time offers, and more. Offer tiered incentives to reward your top referrers and non-monetary motivators proven to increase customer engagement and activation.

Increase Referrals and Boost Sales

Prompt your current customers to share their most recent purchase with a great reward for them, and a promotional offer for their friends, family and colleagues. The reward for your current customer can be automatically triggered and includes smart controls to ensure a valid new purchase. And, you can even provide multi-level referral rewards too.

Deploy Machine Learning for Referrals

Take referrals to the next level by utilizing predictive insights through machine learning to transform how you identify customers likely to refer and engage your top performing referrers. Group your customers into user segments based on their likelihood to refer, and offer incentives personalized to each tier of user for a near-guaranteed boost in referrals.

Demonstrate Your Customer Benefits

Gain a competitive edge by leveraging the power of data. Administer the value tracker workflow to automatically track, calculate and display the overall lifetime benefit of every promotion and discount offer used by your customers, including their referral rewards. Plus, you can always include a call to action to continue referring for even greater rewards.

Boost Your Referrals with Sweepstakes

With the sweepstakes workflow, you can now increase excitement and create a near guaranteed boost of referrals for your business. Create a structure that rewards customers with a sweepstake entry for every referral. Then, add a separate sweepstakes, featuring a great reward, with entries reserved only for customers whose referrals proved successful.

Create a Successful, Buzz Worthy Experience

Encourage valuable connections at trade shows and boost interest and attendance for your own online and in-person events by deploying the events workflows. Motivate attendees to rate, review, and refer new products and just launched services, and invite them to share their experiences with multiple referral touchpoints for exciting and successful events.

Turn Announcements into Opportunities

Do more than inform your customers, engage them with the newsfeed workflow and generate even more referrals. The workflow delivers important information exactly where and how you want, along with a gamified incentive for sharing, such as a premier profile badge. Plus, you can include polling to boost interest, short quizzes for information capture, and much more.

Power Referrals with Personalized Redemption Links

Provide a choice of incentives to increase the quality and quantity of referrals. From no-cost contest and sweepstakes entries, to your own low-cost branded merchandise, discounts on future purchases, gift cards and other direct cash incentives, and even big ticket items - the workflow makes it easy for you to always include the most effective incentive.
